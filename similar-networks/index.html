Start Your Own Mesh Network

Essential steps to build a thriving mesh community in your area

Community Building Essentials

  1. Organize people first - The hardest (and most fun) part is finding enthusiasts. You need many people to set up nodes around town.
  2. Hold regular meetings - We meet quarterly. Sometimes 30+ people, sometimes just 2-3 at the pub. Regular schedule builds confidence.
  3. Set up online chat - Use Discord, Slack, or Telegram alongside the mesh for coordination.
  4. Create an email list - Not everyone uses chat apps. Email ensures broad communication.

Outreach & Partnerships

  1. Social media presence - Advertise meetings and celebrate achievements. Share your solar node setups!
  2. Connect with ham radio clubs - They're radio experts with access to towers and rooftops.
  3. Partner with disaster relief - Red Cross and mutual aid groups often welcome repeaters on their buildings.

Growth Strategy: Drone Operations

Weekly coordinated drone flights can dramatically expand your network reach and discover hidden nodes.

The strategy: Use a node on the ground set to client_mute to send messages inviting people to your chat room and generate mesh traffic.

Why it works: Many people have nodes sitting on desks because they saw no activity when first powered on. Your message may not be seen immediately, but will be discovered over time.

Pro tip: In larger meshes (200+ online nodes), configure drone nodes as ROUTER.

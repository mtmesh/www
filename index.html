Why MTMesh?

Resilient Communication

Stay connected even when power grids fail or cell towers go down. Our solar-powered mesh network operates independently.

Community Driven

Built by Montanans for Montanans. Every node strengthens the network and helps connect rural communities.

No Monthly Fees

Free text messaging forever. Once you have a compatible device, communication costs nothing.

Privacy Focused

End-to-end encryption and no corporate surveillance. Your conversations stay private.

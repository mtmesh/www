    Educational Partnerships

    MTMesh is actively seeking partnerships with Montana schools, universities, and educational organizations to bring hands-on STEM learning opportunities to students across the state. We believe in empowering young minds to explore wireless communication, electronics, and programming using Meshtastic devices.

    STEM Learning with Meshtastic Technology

    We're developing educational programs to introduce Montana students to real-world applications of wireless mesh networking. Using open-source LoRa (Long Range) technology, students can engage in hands-on activities that foster problem-solving, teamwork, and innovation while learning about:

    • Fundamentals of wireless communication technology
    • Practical experience in electronics and coding through building mesh networks
    • Critical thinking and collaboration skills via real-world problem-solving
    • Career exploration in technology, communication, and software development

    Partnership Opportunities

    We're looking for Montana educational institutions interested in:

    • K-12 Schools: Integrate Meshtastic technology into STEM curricula and after-school programs
    • Universities & Colleges: Research partnerships and student project opportunities
    • Libraries & Community Centers: Host educational workshops and maker spaces
    • Amateur Radio Clubs: Collaborate on technical education and emergency communications

    Interested in bringing STEM opportunities to your Montana organization? Contact us to discuss partnership possibilities!

    Community & Business Partnerships

    MTMesh welcomes partnerships with Montana businesses, municipalities, and community organizations to expand our mesh network coverage and provide valuable services to communities across the state.

    Partnership Benefits

    • Enhanced Communication: Backup communications during emergencies or network outages
    • Community Resilience: Support local emergency preparedness and disaster response
    • Technical Innovation: Participate in cutting-edge mesh networking technology
    • Educational Impact: Support STEM education in Montana communities

    How You Can Partner

    • Host a Node: Provide rooftop or tower space for mesh network repeaters
    • Sponsor Equipment: Support network expansion through equipment donations
    • Educational Support: Host workshops or provide expertise to community programs
    • Emergency Services: Integrate mesh communications into emergency response plans

    Ready to explore partnership opportunities with MTMesh? Reach out today to learn how we can work together to strengthen Montana communities through mesh networking.
